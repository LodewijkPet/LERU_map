# Liechtenstein deep-dive sources

Access date: 28 April 2026

## Initial app profile and committee/case-file seed pass

### Seed bodies

- Body: Office of Education
- Type: national higher-education authority
- Lane: system frame, approval and recognition
- Role: official page lists the University of Liechtenstein and UFL as the two state-recognized universities with degree-awarding powers and the Liechtenstein Institute as a university-like research institution without degree-awarding powers.
- Strongest current source: https://www.llv.li/en/national-administration/office-of-education/educational-areas/education-schools/higher-education
- Case-file status: not a research-misconduct archive.

- Body: University of Liechtenstein Research Support Office and independent ombudsperson
- Type: institutional research-integrity route
- Lane: suspected scientific misconduct and good scientific practice
- Role: public route for advice, confidential reporting, initial assessment, investigation/hearing and consequences.
- Strongest current source: https://www.uni.li/en/research/research-funding/research-strategy/integrity
- Publication class: procedure only.
- Case-file status: no public case archive, anonymized decision page or annual ombudsperson statistics located in this first pass.

- Body: UFL academic-integrity ombudsperson and Disciplinary Board route
- Type: institutional research-integrity route
- Lane: good scientific practice and suspected scientific misconduct
- Role: UFL page names the ombudsperson as an independent and neutral body that receives and investigates information and can pursue measures with the Disciplinary Board.
- Strongest current sources: https://www.ufl.li/en/research/research-at-ufl and https://www.ufl.li/fileadmin/user_upload/00_Reglemente/2025-08-20_Regeln_zur_Sicherung_guter_wissenschaftlicher_Praxis_V3.pdf
- Publication class: procedure only.
- Case-file status: no public case archive located.

- Body: Liechtenstein Institute
- Type: university-like research institution
- Lane: research governance and publication
- Role: statute defines the Institute as a scientific research and academic teaching institution, with project approval by competent organs and publication recommendations by the Scientific Council.
- Strongest current source: https://liechtenstein-institut.li/Portals/0/docs/Statuten/Statut%2011.pdf
- Publication class: governance and publication route, not misconduct handling.

- Body: Office of Public Health and Zurich Cantonal Ethics Committee
- Type: biomedical boundary route
- Lane: clinical trials and medical-device clinical investigations
- Role: official page says medical-device clinical trials in Liechtenstein require Zurich ethics review and, for relevant categories, Office of Public Health authorisation or notification.
- Strongest current source: https://www.llv.li/en/business/industry-professional-associations/health-professions-and-remedies/medical-devices-in-vitro-diagnostics/clinical-exams
- Case-file status: approval and reporting lane, not general misconduct publication.

- Body: Office for Food Control and Veterinary Affairs
- Type: animal-research boundary route
- Lane: animal experiments and laboratory-animal keeping, breeding or trade
- Role: official page says animal experiments require permits and each proposed animal experiment is assessed by a cantonal animal-experiment commission.
- Strongest current source: https://www.llv.li/de/unternehmen/branchen-berufe-verbaende/tierhaltung-und-tierbetreuung/bewilligungspflichtige-taetigkeiten-mit-tieren
- Case-file status: authorisation route; no misconduct archive located.
