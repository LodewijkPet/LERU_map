# Slovenia deep-dive sources

Accessed 21 April 2026 for the Slovenia deep-dive pass.

## National legal and council layer

- Scientific Research and Innovation Activity Act (ZZrID): https://pisrs.si/api/datoteke/integracije/358724984
  Used for Articles 39-41, including the duties of research organizations, the national council, and the open-science frame.
- Resolution on the Slovenian Scientific Research and Innovation Strategy 2030: https://www.gov.si/assets/ministrstva/MVZI/Znanost/Nacionalne-strategije-in-dokumenti/Resolution-on-the-Slovenian-Scientific-Research-and-Innovation-Strategy-2030.pdf
  Used for the national strategy timeline and for the link between ethics, integrity, open science and research assessment.
- National Council for Ethics and Integrity in Science page: https://www.gov.si/zbirke/delovna-telesa/delovna-skupina/
  Main hub for mandate, membership, key documents and the public work-report link.
- Public launch notice for the National Council: https://www.gov.si/novice/2024-03-25-vzpostavljen-nacionalni-svet-za-etiko-in-integriteto-v-znanosti/
  Used for the constitutive meeting date, mandate framing and first public membership list.
- Rules of operation of the National Council: https://www.gov.si/assets/ministrstva/MVZI/Znanost/Etika-in-integriteta-v-znanosti/Poslovnik-o-delu-Nacionalnega-sveta-za-etiko-in-integriteto-v-znanosti.pdf
  Used for quorum, non-public sittings and ministry support.
- Procedure rules for adopting opinions: https://www.gov.si/assets/ministrstva/MVZI/Znanost/Etika-in-integriteta-v-znanosti/Pravila-postopka-za-sprejemanje-mnenj-o-skladnosti-ravnanja-z-eticnimi-pravili-in-o-krsitvah-nacel-raziskovalne-integritete.pdf
  Key source for showing that the council usually stands above institutional committees rather than replacing them.
- Recommendations to research and higher-education organizations: https://www.gov.si/assets/ministrstva/MVZI/Znanost/Etika-in-integriteta-v-znanosti/Priporocila-raziskovalnim-in-visokosolskim-organizacijam-glede-ureditve-podrocja-raziskovalne-integritete.pdf
  Used to code the expected local architecture: codes, public procedures, committees and whistleblower protection.
- Work report 2023-2025 of the National Council: https://www.gov.si/assets/ministrstva/MVZI/Znanost/Etika-in-integriteta-v-znanosti/Porocilo-o-delu-Nacionalnega-sveta-za-etiko-in-integriteto-v-znanosti-2023-2025.pdf
  Core source for the council's activity record, ENRIO positioning and the explicit statement that the council cannot itself impose sanctions.

## Open science and ARIS

- Government article on open-science implementation: https://www.gov.si/novice/2023-06-06-vlada-odprla-poti-za-uresnicevanje-odprte-znanosti/
  Used for the dates of the 2023 decree and action plan.
- Decree on the implementation of scientific research work in accordance with the principles of open science: https://www.gov.si/assets/ministrstva/MVZI/Znanost/Obzorje-Evropa/Novice/2023-Open-Science/Decree-on-the-implementation-of-scientific-research-work-open-science.pdf
  Main source for funder obligations, repository rules, DMPs and FAIR-data requirements.
- Action Plan for Open Science: https://www.gov.si/assets/ministrstva/MVZI/Znanost/Nacionalne-strategije-in-dokumenti/2023-Action-Plan-Open-Science-Slovenia.pdf
  Used to map implementation timelines and ARIS participation.
- ARIS mission and vision: https://www.aris-rs.si/en/agencija/mission.asp
  Used to confirm that ARIS follows policies of open science and scientific integrity.
- ARIS criteria for trusted repositories: https://www.aris-rs.si/sl/akti/24/merila-repozitoriji-dec24.asp
  Used as a concrete implementation trace of the decree.

### Classification cautions

- Slovenian sources frequently combine ethics and integrity in the same body title. Do not assume every public output is a research-integrity case.
- National Council opinions, if issued, can be integrity opinions or ethical-rule opinions; classify each opinion by topic before aggregating.
- University of Ljubljana exposes a research-misconduct reporting route, while FOV exposes research-ethics approval identifiers. These are different publication classes.
- National Medical Ethics Committee and animal-procedure sources are boundary approval routes, not general misconduct publication.
- The 2023-2025 work report is important negative-publication evidence: it shows one ARIS-referred initiative and no proceeding, plus the lack of direct sanction power.
