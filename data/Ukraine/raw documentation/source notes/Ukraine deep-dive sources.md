## Gaps to revisit

- Public sources are not fully aligned on the start date of the Law On Academic Integrity: the current Rada page points to 31 July 2026, while NASU and NAQA materials describe implementation from 1 February 2026.
- NAQA is clearly a national second-line route with a public complaint/decision page; a later sweep should index the page by year, stage and outcome.
- More NASU institutes and universities should be added beyond KNU, KPI, NaUKMA and the NASU/KPI micro-publication examples found on 24 April 2026.
- The clinical-ethics boundary now has a cleaner DEC landing page, but a later sweep can still add current trial-status or committee-list sources if needed.
- The animal-research boundary now has direct Rada/MON legal sources; public case-file visibility for this route remains separate and limited.

## Committee and case-file pass, 24 April 2026

### Main finding

Ukraine has a clearer public case route than the first deep-dive text implied. NAQA has a live public page for complaints/reports concerning academic plagiarism, fabrication and falsification that were accepted for Ethics Committee consideration. The page groups complaint entries and links supporting PDFs, acceptance decisions and, for some matters, reasoned decision PDFs. This should be coded as a structured national second-line case page, not merely as procedure-only visibility.

The important limit remains scope: the NAQA page is not a universal national archive of every institutional research-integrity case. It is strongest for the NAQA second-line dissertation, degree and academic-work route. Local university and research-institution outcomes remain distributed and are only selectively visible.

### Updated gaps

- Index the NAQA complaint/decision page by year, complaint stage and outcome instead of treating it as a single generic source.
- Monitor how the Law On Academic Integrity's institutional publication duties work after the 2026 legal transition, especially the five-working-day publication rule for relevant institutional decisions.
- Widen academy and institutional examples beyond NASU and KPI, including NASU institutes and large universities with research-specific misconduct policies or public minutes.
- Keep clinical-trial ethics, animal-research, data-protection and IP routes as boundary regimes even when their official source trail is clearer than some local academic-integrity routes.
