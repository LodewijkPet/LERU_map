## Committee and case-file pass

Access date: 24 April 2026.

### Seed bodies

- Body: Committee for Ethics in Science / Odbor za etiku u nauci.
- Type: statutory national research-integrity committee route.
- Lane: national code, scientific-title, quality-body and public-funding consequence lane.
- Role: the Law on Science and Research and 2023 Code of Conduct route alleged breaches through a national committee that can issue public warnings, start title-revocation procedures, propose exclusion from science-quality bodies and propose a five-year project-funding ban.
- Strongest current source: Law on Science and Research, article 93-95 route, plus the ministry Science and Research page and the National Council 2024 state-of-science report dated 17 September 2025.
- Publication owner: no separate publication owner found; the law names the committee, title bodies and ministry as route actors.
- Archive scope: no public decision archive or warning archive located.
- Database function: none located for misconduct cases.
- Micro-publication node: ministry report hub, but this is implementation reporting rather than case output.
- Individual case visibility: procedure only.
- Public data elements: statutory powers, composition rule, appointment rule, title-revocation referral path and consequence menu.
- Non-public or restricted elements: any complaint file, evidence file, committee conclusion, warning text, title-revocation request file or ministry consequence decision was not located as public material.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: the law and code are real misconduct-route sources, but they do not expose any individual decision, warning or case summary.
- Archive continuity: none found.
- Referral path: requests on title revocation enter through the Committee for Ethics in Science; title bodies decide revocation and the ministry decides some quality-body and funding consequences.
- Publication depth: procedure-only.
- Publicity mode: proactive publication of law/code/report documents; no proactive case publication found.
- Publication lifecycle: not identified for case outputs.
- Outcome maturity: statutory route without located public outcomes.
- Route scope versus archive scope: broad national route, no public archive.
- Publication class: procedure only.
- Accessibility status: law/code/ministry/report sources live; case archive not found.
- Case-file status: no public trace found.
- Publication basis: statutory route exists; publication basis for warnings or decisions not located beyond the term "public warning" in the consequence menu.
- Publication exclusions: not identified.
- Publication rule or retention: not identified.
