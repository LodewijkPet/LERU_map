# Croatia deep-dive sources

Accessed 22 April 2026 for the Croatia deep-dive pass.

## National law and Council layer

- Law on Higher Education and Scientific Activity: https://narodne-novine.nn.hr/clanci/sluzbeni/2022_10_119_1834.html
  Core constitutive source for Articles 55, 56 and 86: local ethics committees, ethics codes, disciplinary committees and the National Council's minimum-principles role.
- Law on Scientific Activity and Higher Education: https://narodne-novine.nn.hr/clanci/sluzbeni/2003_07_123_1742.html
  Historical source for the earlier national Ethics Board in science and higher education.
- National Council ministry page: https://mzom.gov.hr/istaknute-teme/nacionalna-vijeca-strucna-i-radna-tijela-i-povjerenstva/nacionalno-vijece-za-visoko-obrazovanje-znanost-i-tehnoloski-razvoj/324
  Main official entry point for the current Council and its statutory competences.
- Decision appointing National Council members: https://mzom.gov.hr/UserDocsImages/dokumenti/StrucnaTijela/Nvvzt/Odluka-o-imenovanju-clanova-NN-81-2023.pdf
  Official appointment trace for the current national body.
- Constitutive session of the National Council: https://nvvoztr.gov.hr/vijesti/odrzana-konstituirajuca-sjednica-nacionalnog-vijeca/66
  Used for the practical activation date of the current Council.
- National Council documents page: https://nvvoztr.gov.hr/izvjesca/10
  Public trace for minutes, reports and other Council documents.
- Annual report of the National Council 2024-2025: https://nvvoztr.gov.hr/UserDocsImages/dokumenti/Godi%C5%A1nje%20izvje%C5%A1%C4%87e%20Nacionalnog%20vije%C4%87a_2024-2025.pdf
  Used to confirm that the Council is active and that minimum ethical principles still appear as a live work item rather than a clearly closed document set.
- Consultation report on the 2022 reform law: https://esavjetovanja.gov.hr/ECon/EconReport?Order=Descending&SortBy=SliceSortOrder&UserIdFilter=2969&entityId=20457
  Important official reform-logic source showing the move away from a centralized ethics-breach model.

## Quality assurance, registers and open science

- Act on Quality Assurance in Higher Education and Science: https://narodne-novine.nn.hr/clanci/sluzbeni/2022_12_151_2330.html
  Legal source for internal quality assurance, external evaluation and reaccreditation.
- AZVO re-accreditation page: https://www.azvo.hr/en/evaluation/re-accreditation/
  Used to confirm the five-year reaccreditation cycle for higher education institutions and research institutes.
- MZOM Register of Higher Education Institutions: https://mzom.gov.hr/istaknute-teme/odgoj-i-obrazovanje/visoko-obrazovanje/upisnik-visokih-ucilista-161/161
  Official public register entry point.
- CroRIS official records page: https://www.croris.hr/upisnici/?lang=en
  Used to confirm the public register layer, including the note that the Register of Research Organizations was abolished on 22 October 2022.
- Croatian Plan for Open Science: https://mzom.gov.hr/UserDocsImages/dokumenti/Znanost/Otvorena-znanost/Hrvatski-plan-za-otvorenu-znanost-svibanj-2025-MZOM.pdf
  Main national source for open science, responsible evaluation and scientific-integrity-adjacent transparency work.

  Existing deep-dive source re-read for the case-file pass. It is the best current institutional statistics source found in Croatia, but it is not a decision archive.
- University of Rijeka Vijece casti statement on irregularities: https://uniri.hr/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/2024_02_12_Vijece_casti_Priopcenje.pdf
  Representative institutional public statement source. It helps code Rijeka as a micro-publication node rather than a full case archive.
- University of Rijeka Vijece casti page: https://uniri.hr/o-sveucilistu/struktura/vijece-casti/
  Existing deep-dive source re-read as the main public page for opinions, statements and code-implementation material.

Case-file findings:

- The strongest national case-file trail is historical: the old Odbor produced public annual reports and at least some individual opinions.
- The current National Council is a standard-setting and reporting body, not a public misconduct-decision archive.
- Zagreb is the strongest current institutional case-trace source because annual reports expose appeals, constituent-unit volumes and outcomes, but not a searchable decision database.
- Rijeka is a current institutional micro-publication node with public opinions and statements.
- HRZZ has a clear Committee for Scientific Integrity route in its code, but no public HRZZ opinion or decision archive was located in this pass.
- Split's research-integrity action plan envisages complaint handling and anonymized annual reporting, but a live output page was not located.
- HALMED, biomedical ethics, animal research, AZOP and IP routes remain boundary regimes and should not upgrade the general misconduct-publication rating.

## Gaps to revisit

- The current dossier has enough National Council evidence to code the system, but a later sweep should capture a cleaner set of annual-report and consultation-report extracts.
- Minimum ethical principles are clearly a statutory task of the National Council, but later checking should confirm whether a finalized document was adopted after the current public reporting trail.
- More public institutes and universities should be checked beyond Zagreb, Rijeka, Split and IRB, especially for annual reports, anonymized summaries, opinions and statements.
- The animal-research boundary is real, but a cleaner direct ministry or veterinary-authority page would improve the national route.
- No current national archive of research-integrity decisions was identified; current public visibility remains largely institutional and document-based.
