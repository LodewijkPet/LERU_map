## Preliminary system reading

Andorra's first visible layer is higher-education quality assurance and research assessment rather than a separate national research-misconduct system. AQUA appears to be the key national body for higher-education quality, while the University of Andorra is likely the first institutional source to screen for research ethics and integrity rules.

The first working hypothesis is therefore: compact higher-education system with quality-assurance governance, institutional research policy, and a still-unresolved research-misconduct layer.

### System reading after expansion

Andorra should currently be coded as a compact, distributed route-visible system rather than as a country with a single national research-misconduct board.

The strongest public national corridor is quality assurance. AQUA evaluates, accredits and certifies higher-education quality and also covers research-quality evaluation in higher education institutions. Its public materials show evaluation processes, an appeal route, annual-report tables, expert/commission governance and an academic-integrity research line. These are important for public accountability, but they are QA and research-quality sources, not general misconduct decisions.

The strongest institutional research-governance route is the University of Andorra. The Research and Doctoral Commission manages research policy, lines, groups, projects and doctoral monitoring. The doctoral programme page records supervisor accreditation, milestones and external evaluation. The Quality Council page and April 2024 plagiarism/fraud procedure expose a public academic-integrity procedure, but the located scope is mostly teaching and assessment fraud, not a public research-misconduct case archive.

Government research support is visible through the Department of Higher Education, Research and Technological Innovation, including third-cycle grants, research-support calls and research dissemination. Andorra Recerca + Innovacio is a public-sector research and innovation foundation created by Law 11/2021 and should be mapped as a research actor and possible future policy source, not as an adjudication body.

Biomedical research is now clearly a separate boundary lane. Law 3/2023 and the Ministry of Health CEI page create a national route for clinical and biomedical research project review and validation. Data protection and IP are also boundary lanes through APDA and OMPA.

### Route and case-publication findings

- AQUA evaluations and annual reports: public QA corridor. AQUA's 2024 activity report lists programme evaluations, including favourable, unfavourable and pending outcomes, and records an academic-integrity research line involving Andorran higher-education institutions. This is decision-like QA evidence, not a misconduct archive.
- AQUA appeals: public procedure. The appeals page and appeal guide describe the Commission of Appeals route for administrative appeals against AQUA evaluation resolutions. No public appeal-decision archive was located in this pass.
- University of Andorra Research and Doctoral Commission: research-governance route. Public page gives composition and functions including research lines, groups, projects, coordination and doctoral monitoring. No public RI case output found.
- University of Andorra Quality Council plagiarism/fraud procedure: public procedure for plagiarism and academic fraud in evaluable activities. Treat as academic-integrity/teaching evidence unless a thesis, publication or research-project case link is found.
- Government research grants: funder route. Current third-cycle grants and research dissemination pages show funding and public research-community support. No public grant-misconduct sanction list found.
- Ministry of Health CEI: biomedical ethics boundary. The CEI evaluates and validates clinical and biomedical research in Andorra; no approval list, minutes stream or case archive found.
- APDA resolutions: boundary decision page. Public data-protection resolutions and instructions exist, but they are not research-misconduct findings.
- OMPA: IP boundary route. Trademark and patent registration, gazettes and agent routes are visible; classify separately from authorship/integrity issues unless a case source links them.
