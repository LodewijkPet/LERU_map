# Belarus deep-dive sources

Accessed 22 April 2026 for the Belarus deep-dive pass.

## State legal and governance layer

- Science in Belarus: https://president.gov.by/en/belarus/science
  Main official framing source for the national science system, including NASB, SCST and VAK.
- State Committee on Science and Technology: https://president.gov.by/en/statebodies/state-committee-for-science-and-technology
  Official state-body framing for SCST.
- NASB tasks and functions: https://nasb.gov.by/eng/about/status-zadachi/
  Best source for the Academy's role as the highest state scientific organization.
- NASB accreditation page: https://nasb.gov.by/eng/activity/spravochnaya-informatsiya/
  Used to confirm that accreditation of scientific organizations sits jointly with NASB and SCST and still cites the Law On Scientific Activity.
- NASB normative base: https://nasb.gov.by/eng/about/normativnaya-baza/
  Main legal hub listing the constitutive laws and the 2002 public-administration decree.
- Charter of the NASB: https://nasb.gov.by/reference/UstavNANB.pdf
  Official charter and governance basis.
- Law On the Foundations of State Scientific and Technical Policy: https://pravo.by/document/?guid=3871&p0=V19302105
  Constitutive law named by NASB.
- Law On Scientific Activity: https://pravo.by/document/?guid=3871&p0=V19600708
  Main law cited in official accreditation materials.
- Law On the National Academy of Sciences of Belarus: https://pravo.by/document/?guid=3871&p0=H19800159
  Core legal source for the Academy.
- NASB Code of Professional Ethics of an Employee: https://nasb.gov.by/rus/information/the_code.pdf
  Key academy-level ethics source; needs careful contextual reading because it blends recognizable integrity norms with confidentiality and loyalty language.
- Department of Science and Innovation: https://edu.gov.by/en-uk/nauka-i-innovatsii/upravlenie-nauki-i-innovatsionnoy-deyatelnosti/
  Official Ministry of Education route for the science and innovation administration layer.

## Committee and case-file pass

Accessed 24 April 2026 for the Belarus committee-and-case-file pilot.

### Seed bodies

- Body: Higher Attestation Commission of the Republic of Belarus (VAK).
  Type: National attestation and dissertation-control authority.
  Lane: Degree, academic-title, dissertation-council and appeal route.
  Role: The clearest national integrity-like control lane, because VAK can regulate dissertation councils, confirm or reject degree/title outcomes, and handle deprivation, restoration and appeals under the official attestation rules.
  Strongest current source: Presidential Decree No. 560 of 16 December 2013 on VAK (`https://pravo.by/document/?guid=3871&p0=P31300560`) and Presidential Decree No. 560 of 17 November 2004 on awarding degrees and conferring titles (`https://pravo.by/document/?guid=3871&p0=P30400560`).
  Publication owner: VAK and the official legal-information system for rules; the live `vak.gov.by` host remains an access/stability problem from this network.
  Archive scope: The known VAK library target (`https://vak.gov.by/library`) is an author-abstract/dissertation metadata target, not a misconduct-decision archive.
  Database function: Dissertation metadata or author-abstract library; procedure source for revocation/restoration/appeals.
  Individual case visibility: No public VAK misconduct, degree-deprivation or appeal decision archive was located in this pass.
  Public data elements: Legal powers, procedure categories, appeal route, dissertation metadata target.
  Non-public or restricted elements: Full attestation files, expert council files, appeal materials, evidence files and case reasoning.
  Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: VAK is the strongest national route for dissertation-integrity consequences, but the public material located in this pass is rule and metadata visibility rather than reasoned case decisions.
  Publication class: Procedure only / metadata target.
  Accessibility status: Pravo links are official source targets but automated access can be blocked; `vak.gov.by` timed out in this pass.
  Case-file status: No public case archive found.

- Body: NASB and SCST accreditation route.
  Type: State science-governance and competence-recognition route.
  Lane: Accreditation and scientific-organization competence.
  Role: National governance layer that can shape integrity expectations but does not present as a complaint or case-publication route.
  Strongest current source: NASB accreditation page and NASB tasks/functions page.
  Individual case visibility: No case-level accreditation or misconduct archive found.
  Publication class: Procedure and governance visibility.

- Body: Belarusian State Economic University Expert Council for dissertations.
  Type: Institutional dissertation-control route.
  Lane: Pre-defense and institutional expert review.
  Role: Local dissertation-quality and personal-contribution review before or alongside national attestation.
  Strongest current source: BSEU expert council regulation.
  Individual case visibility: No public case outputs found.
  Publication class: Procedure only.

- Body: Belarusian State University ethics / publication-ethics routes.
  Type: Institutional ethics and editorial route.
  Lane: University code route and journal publication-ethics route.
  Role: Public norms, peer review, authorship, plagiarism and editorial-response rules.
  Strongest current source: BSU Ethical Code and BSU History journal about page.
  Individual case visibility: Policy visibility only; no institutional decision archive found.
  Publication class: Procedure only / editorial policy.

- Body: Ministry of Health Bioethics Committee and BSMU ethics committee.
  Type: Biomedical ethics boundary route.
  Lane: Bioethics composition, health-research ethics and medical-university ethics.
  Role: Formal biomedical ethics infrastructure that is more visible than the general misconduct route.
  Strongest current source: Ministry committee-composition update, Ministry legal-base entry and BSMU committee work page.
  Individual case visibility: Committee and route visibility rather than public misconduct decisions.
  Publication class: Procedure and composition visibility.

- Body: Center for Examinations and Tests in Health Service (RCETH).
  Type: Ministry of Health subordinate regulator / boundary register.
  Lane: Clinical-trial permissions, clinical-pharmacological examination and GCP inspection support.
  Role: RCETH exposes a live register of clinical-trial permissions and the Republican Clinical and Pharmacological Laboratory function page says the laboratory supports conclusions for clinical-trial permissions, examines significant protocol amendments and participates in GCP inspections.
  Strongest current source: RCETH register of permissions for clinical studies/trials of medicinal products (`https://www.rceth.by/Refbank/reestr_clinic_test`) and RCETH Republican Clinical and Pharmacological Laboratory functions (`https://www.rceth.by/en/Departments/Rcpl`).
  Archive scope: Clinical-trial permission metadata and regulatory functions; not a general research-misconduct archive.
  Database function: Boundary register.
  Individual case visibility: Study/protocol metadata, phase, status, research bases and permission date fields; no misconduct reasoning or sanctions.
  Publication class: Boundary register.

- Body: Laboratory-animal / responsible-treatment law route.
  Type: Animal-research boundary.
  Lane: Responsible treatment of animals and laboratory-animal definitions or requirements.
  Role: Law-level boundary evidence only in this pass.
  Strongest current source: Law No. 361-Z of 1 April 2024 on responsible treatment of animals (`https://pravo.by/document/?guid=12551&p0=H12400361`).
  Individual case visibility: No public animal-research permission, inspection or case-output archive was located.
  Publication class: Procedure/law only.
