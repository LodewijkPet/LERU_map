## Committee and case-file pass

Date: 24 April 2026

### System reading after repository search

Austria has public case-material, but it is not organized like LOWI or ETH Zurich. The strongest public route is the OeAWI Commission annual-report corridor: the OeAWI download area lists Commission annual reports from 2011 through 2023, and the 2023 report contains numbered anonymized inquiry summaries. This is better than a bare procedure page, but it is still an annual-report archive rather than a searchable decision database.

The institutional sweep found several route and publication-rule fragments, but no ETH-style public institutional case-report list:

- University of Vienna exposes a research-route page, an ombuds route and public thesis/plagiarism statistics; its 2006 ombuds directive permits anonymized case descriptions and annual reporting, but no live public case-report repository was located.
- Medical University of Innsbruck's statute requires anonymized annual activity reporting by its GSP Panel to the rectorate and senate; no public annual-report archive was located in this pass.
- University of Salzburg has a public Commission for Good Scientific Practice page with tasks, exclusions and current composition; no public decisions or annual case reports were located.
- TU Wien, ISTA, FH Campus Wien, WU and other institutions appear in the Austrian higher-education ombuds directory as contact or committee routes; the directory is a route matrix, not case-publication evidence.

### Seed bodies

- Body: OeAWI Commission for Research Integrity
- Type: National membership-based investigation route
- Lane: Suspected research misconduct with a strong Austrian institutional or researcher link
- Role: Receives reports from member organizations or affected individuals, checks responsibility, conducts preliminary or main investigations, obtains statements and expert opinions, may support mediation, and issues final statements with recommendations.
- Strongest current source: OeAWI investigation page, organs page and OeAWI download area.
- Publication owner: OeAWI.
- Archive scope: Commission annual reports, with anonymized case narratives and statistics; no case-by-case decision database located.
- Database function: Annual-report corridor, not a searchable decision archive.
- Individual case visibility: Numbered anonymized inquiries, allegations, procedure route, outcome and recommendation level.
- Public data elements: year, inquiry number, broad institution type, misconduct category, route, whether proceedings were opened or closed, anonymized reasoning and recommendations.
- Non-public or restricted elements: names, full final statement, evidence file, hearings, correspondence and implementing institutional sanctions.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: The annual reports provide real case summaries and are the strongest Austrian public case-material source, but the Commission says confidentiality is central and the final statement is conveyed to affected persons or institutions.

- Body: FWF
- Type: National funder route
- Lane: FWF applications, grants and funded projects
- Role: Investigates suspected academic misconduct using its own procedure and in cooperation with OeAWI; the Executive Board acts through the FWF Board route.
- Strongest current source: FWF academic-integrity page and FWF procedure PDF.
- Publication owner: FWF.
- Archive scope: FWF states that investigation results are published annually; no separate case archive was located.
- Database function: Funder annual-report / statistics corridor.
- Individual case visibility: Aggregate or annual-result visibility only in this pass.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: FWF is a real sanction-adjacent route, but the public trace is annualized rather than a decision repository.

- Body: University of Vienna Ombuds Office and Standing Commission
- Type: Institutional ombuds and commission route
- Lane: Scientific misconduct by University of Vienna researchers, plus a separate thesis and study-integrity route
- Role: Ombudspersons advise, check reports and refer cases to the Standing Commission; the Commission decides whether misconduct is established and reports to the rectorate with possible consequences.
- Strongest current source: University of Vienna good-scientific-practice page and the 31 January 2006 ombuds directive.
- Publication owner: University of Vienna.
- Archive scope: Publication basis only. The directive permits anonymized annual case descriptions and anonymized case representations; no public repository located.
- Micro-publication node: The public page gives study/thesis plagiarism statistics since 2005, but this belongs to academic integrity in study and degree revocation rather than general research-misconduct case files.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: The route is real and publication is possible, but public case files were not found; thesis-plagiarism statistics should stay in the study-integrity boundary lane.

- Body: Medical University of Innsbruck GSP Panel
- Type: Institutional good-scientific-practice panel
- Lane: Research misconduct and good scientific practice in a medical-university setting
- Role: Preliminary examination, GSP Panel investigation, written decision and recommendations to the rectorate.
- Strongest current source: Statute section safeguarding good scientific practice.
- Publication owner: Medical University of Innsbruck.
- Archive scope: Internal annual activity report to rectorate and senate with anonymized compilation of suspicions and cases; no public archive found.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: The annual-report rule is case-relevant but internal/publication status is not the same as OeAWI's public annual reports.

- Body: University of Salzburg Commission for Good Scientific Practice
- Type: Institutional commission route
- Lane: University of Salzburg scientific-integrity reports
- Role: Mediates principles of good scientific practice, advises, examines indications of possible misconduct and initiates investigation procedures. It excludes anonymous/external reports and qualification-work reports from its ordinary scope, with referrals to OeAWI or rectorate routes.
- Strongest current source: University of Salzburg commission page.
- Publication owner: University of Salzburg.
- Archive scope: Procedure and composition page; no public case archive found.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: Good route evidence, but no public decisions or case summaries located.
