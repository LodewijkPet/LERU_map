# San Marino deep-dive sources

## First app dossier pass

Access date: 28 April 2026.

San Marino is now treated as an app-profile candidate rather than a data-folder-only placeholder. The sources point to a compact, institution-led system:

- UniRSM is the main higher-education and research institution.
- Law No. 69/2023 and Delegated Decree No. 169/2023 provide the higher-education and university-organization frame.
- UniRSM's Code of Ethics and the 2026 Collegio di Disciplina regulation are the clearest institutional conduct and disciplinary routes.
- The San Marino Bioethics Committee (CSB) and the Research and Experimentation Ethics Committee (CERS) are strong biomedical ethics and clinical-research routes, with public documents, opinions, committee composition and application forms.
- The Data Protection Authority, USBM, Authority Sanitaria, and animal-experimentation law are boundary routes and should not be collapsed into a general research-misconduct archive.

No national general research-misconduct board, public UniRSM misconduct case archive, or public CERS clinical-study approval register was located in this pass.

## Source map

### Higher-education and UniRSM route

- UniRSM Legge Quadro e Statuto page: official synthesis and links for Law No. 69/2023, Delegated Decree No. 169/2023 and the university statute. Used for the national higher-education frame and EHEA/ESG alignment.
- Delegated Decree No. 169/2023 on UniRSM institutional and organizational structure: used for the university governance and statute route.
- UniRSM Code of Ethics page and PDF: used for integrity values, IP, conflicts of interest and disciplinary-responsibility signals.
- UniRSM Collegio di Disciplina regulation, issued by Rectoral Decree No. 01/2026 after approval on 16 December 2025: used for professor/researcher disciplinary procedure and the explicit non-public decision rule.
- UniRSM Research page: used for research centres, third-party funded projects, publications and national/international research positioning.
- UniRSM Quality Assurance page and External Accreditation page: used for research/third-mission quality assurance, PQA/OIV actors, ESG alignment and the first external IEP review.

### Biomedical, clinical and health-research route

- Law No. 39/2023 reforming the CSB: used as the constitutive source for the bioethics and biomedical-research route.
- CSB/CERS official site: used for mission, committee composition, documents, opinions, forms and meeting-calendar/publication surface.
- CERS committee and forms pages: used for clinical/observational study authorization, conflict-of-interest declarations, protocol files, minors' consent files and study documentation.
- CSB documents and opinions pages: used as a public archive of bioethics documents and opinions. These are case-like for bioethics questions, not general research-misconduct decisions.
- Authority Sanitaria page and Ufficio Segreteria Tecnico-Amministrativa page: used for the health-authority and committee-secretariat route.

### Boundary routes

- San Marino Data Protection Authority provvedimenti page: used as a structured data-protection decision archive for boundary coding.
- USBM Innovations page: used for industrial R&D, EUREKA and innovation-funding route evidence.
- USBM office page: used for patent, trademark and design registration boundary evidence.
- Law No. 108/2007 on the ban on animal experimentation: used for the animal-research boundary.
- Law No. 40/2023 on information and media rights: used only for the general freedom of scientific research and teaching baseline.

## Committee and case-file pass

### Seed bodies

- Body: Universita degli Studi della Repubblica di San Marino
- Type: Institution
- Lane: Higher education, research, ethics and discipline
- Role: First-line university route for code compliance, professor/researcher discipline, research quality and academic governance.
- Strongest current source: Code of Ethics page/PDF and Collegio di Disciplina regulation.
- Publication owner: UniRSM.
- Archive scope: Procedure and governance documents only.
- Database function: None found.
- Individual case visibility: No public UniRSM misconduct case files located.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: The sources define conduct and discipline routes but do not publish case outcomes.
- Publication class: Procedure only.
- Accessibility status: Live.
- Case-file status: No public trace found for individual misconduct decisions.
- Publication basis: The disciplinary regulation states that the Council decision on sanction or archiving is not public.

- Body: Comitato Sammarinese di Bioetica / Comitato Etico per la Ricerca e la Sperimentazione
- Type: Biomedical ethics and clinical-research route
- Lane: Bioethics, clinical trials, observational studies, pharmacological and epidemiological research.
- Role: Advisory and authorization route for biomedical research and ethical questions.
- Strongest current source: CSB/CERS official site, Law No. 39/2023, CERS forms and CSB document/opinion archives.
- Publication owner: CSB/CERS site and health-sector pages.
- Archive scope: Bioethics documents and opinions; clinical-study forms and committee information.
- Database function: Opinion/document archive; no study-approval register found.
- Individual case visibility: CSB publishes opinion documents, but no CERS clinical-approval decision list was located.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: The sources are biomedical ethics and approval-route outputs, not general research-misconduct findings.
- Publication class: Stable case page / procedure only by lane.
- Accessibility status: Live.
- Case-file status: Boundary case-like archive for bioethics; no general RI archive.

- Body: San Marino Data Protection Authority
- Type: Boundary regulator
- Lane: Personal-data protection.
- Role: Publishes provvedimenti by year and can affect research-data and biomedical-data processing.
- Strongest current source: Provvedimenti archive.
- Publication owner: Data Protection Authority.
- Archive scope: Data-protection decisions from 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022 and 2024.
- Database function: Decision archive.
- Individual case visibility: Public data-protection orders and opinions.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: It is a regulatory data-protection archive, not a research-misconduct route.
- Publication class: Structured archive by boundary lane.
