# North Macedonia deep-dive sources

Access date for this pass: 28 April 2026.

This note starts from `Overview North Macedonia.docx` and the existing deep dossier in `data/countries.js`. It adds the committee-and-case-file pass requested after the Kosovo sweep.

## Committee and case-file pass

### Seed bodies

- Body: Ethics Board under the Law on Scientific-Research Activity
- Type: National statutory ethics body
- Lane: Scientific-research ethics and values
- Role: Monitor and evaluate ethical principles and values in scientific-research activity, human integrity in research and ethical business relations among research actors.
- Strongest current source: Ministry-hosted Law on Scientific-Research Activity, Article 14.
- Publication owner: Government/Ministry route, but no current public Ethics Board page, recommendation page or annual-report page was located in this pass.
- Archive scope: Procedure-only in located public sources.
- Case-file status: No public national misconduct case archive located.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: The law creates a national ethics body and says its opinions/positions are recommendations and that it reports annually to Government, but the located public layer does not expose recommendation files, case decisions or anonymized summaries.

- Body: National Council for Higher Education and Scientific Research Activity
- Type: National quality, standards and member-conduct body
- Lane: Higher-education/scientific-research governance and member ethics
- Role: Publishes standards, programmes, reports, ethical code, ethical-board decision and risk material.
- Strongest current source: National Council homepage/document portal.
- Publication owner: National Council.
- Archive scope: Public document portal.
- Case-file status: Governance/report corridor; not a general misconduct archive.
- Publication class: Procedure and annual/report publication for council work; member-conduct ethical-board route only.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: The Ethical Code creates a three-member Ethical Board for National Council member conduct, complaint handling, warnings, reports and notice to the council, but it does not create a public repository of general researcher misconduct cases.

- Body: Agency for Quality in Higher Education (AQHE), Evaluation Board and Accreditation Board
- Type: Quality-assurance agency and board network
- Lane: External evaluation, self-evaluation, accreditation and doctoral-mentor accreditation
- Role: Publishes evaluation reports and monthly accreditation/mentor decision lists.
- Strongest current source: AQHE Evaluation Board report page and Accreditation Board decision page.
- Publication owner: AQHE.
- Archive scope: Quality-assurance report and decision corridors.
- Case-file status: Strong public QA repository; not a misconduct repository.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: The files can expose ethics, plagiarism, complaint and quality infrastructure, but their decision function is accreditation/evaluation rather than adjudicating research misconduct.

- Body: UKIM central Ethics Commission and unit-level ethics commissions
- Type: Institutional ethics route
- Lane: University code, teachers/associates, students and professional/administrative service
- Role: UKIM Ethical Code creates the University Ethics Commission and unit-level commissions, with report rejection, warning, public warning and referral routes.
- Strongest current source: UKIM Ethical Code page and PDF.
- Publication owner: UKIM.
- Archive scope: Procedure/contact page found; no central public decision archive located.
- Case-file status: Procedure-only centrally; institutional self-evaluation reports provide micro-publication snippets.
- Public data elements: Committee email, code route, disciplinary/plagiarism summaries in some unit self-evaluation reports.
- Non-public or restricted elements: Individual ethics-commission files and central case decisions were not located as a public archive.

- Body: UKIM Faculty of Medicine
- Type: Institutional faculty route and biomedical boundary route
- Lane: Discipline, plagiarism, human-research ethics, animal experiments and research output visibility
- Role: Self-evaluation report describes discipline, plagiarism checks, human-research ethics committee filtering, repository visibility and doctoral-output publication.
- Strongest current source: UKIM Faculty of Medicine self-evaluation report 2023-2024.
- Publication owner: UKIM Faculty of Medicine.
- Archive scope: Self-evaluation report; not a case database.
- Case-file status: Micro-publication / aggregate snippet.
- Representative case-level note: The report states that one disciplinary application was submitted in 2020-2023 for exam irregularities and that the committee imposed a measure barring the student from taking the subject in three successive exam sessions. This is teaching/student discipline, not a research-misconduct decision.

- Body: UKIM Faculty of Technology and Metallurgy (TMF)
- Type: Institutional faculty route
- Lane: Discipline, plagiarism checks and research-ethics forms
- Role: Self-evaluation report describes discipline, plagiarism checks through the ministry system and ethics statements for sensory-evaluation research.
- Strongest current source: UKIM TMF self-evaluation report.
- Publication owner: UKIM/TMF.
- Archive scope: Self-evaluation report; not a case database.
- Case-file status: Micro-publication / aggregate snippet.
- Representative case-level note: The report states that one disciplinary application was submitted in 2020-2024 and that one exclusion measure was imposed for academic year 2020/2021. This is not a general research-misconduct finding.

- Body: South East European University (SEEU)
- Type: Private-university institutional and doctoral route
- Lane: Code of Ethics duty, dispute settlement and Doctoral School Council appeals
- Role: Existing dossier sources show ethics obligations and internal dispute/doctoral appeals, but no public case-output page was found in this pass.
- Strongest current source: SEEU statute and third-cycle rule already in the dossier.
- Publication owner: SEEU.
- Archive scope: Procedure-only in this pass.
- Case-file status: No public case archive located.

- Body: Agency for Personal Data Protection (AZLP)
- Type: Independent data-protection authority
- Lane: Research-data and privacy boundary
- Role: Supervises personal-data processing, handles data-subject requests, performs supervision and publishes selected notices.
- Strongest current source: AZLP organisation page, public-information list, complaint/request page and representative supervision notice.
- Publication owner: AZLP.
- Archive scope: Boundary decision/notice and annual-report corridor.
- Case-file status: Boundary case material only.
- Why this is or is not a misconduct case file: AZLP decisions can be highly case-like and relevant to research data, but they are data-protection files, not research-integrity adjudication.
